Our Services

Adventurous Machines provides the solution to developing the electronics and software for your product, ready for it to be manufactured.

Electronic Engineering

We design, prototype, test and develop the electronics for your product -- either from scratch, or from your existings designs. We will design your product from the start with legislation in mind, and will work through compliance testing with you.

Firmware Development

Modern electronics is nothing without firmware. We employ modern software engineering practices to achieve robust firmware that can be adapted to new challenges as time goes on.

Embedded Systems Engineering

Working to the specific constraints of your product means that we produce designs with wide ranges of capabilities: from resource-constrained microcontrollers to systems on which we can run Linux and language VMs.

Product development

Optimal product specification is hard to come by, therefore we can iterate with you through your lean experiments, and requirements gathering process to rapidly find the best product for you.

Prototyping

We will design, manufacture and test your prototypes with a quick turnaround time, so that you can tackle the crucial questions you need answered.

Design Review

We often find that clients have an internal development team that needs some advice about a product from an external perspective.

Automated Test

Testing is a vital part of the continued development and maintenance of your product, and we design and implement robust and automated testing processes to ensure quality results.

PCB Design

We will design PCBs to fit the mechanical constraints of your product.

New Challenges

We are excited to approach new challenges and are always interested in new technologies and approaches. We will bring new collaborators into our team as needed, and are determined to find the best solution for your product.
